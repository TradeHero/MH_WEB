About TradeHero

Our vision at TradeHero is to democratise the world of investing through disruptive knowledge sharing. We believe everyone can learn to invest when you combine the power of crowdsourced intelligence with a fun, intuitive, and non-threatening platform. That’s why we’ve developed a gamified virtual investment network using real world data that makes it easy to learn by doing, share real time insights, and compete to win real cash, making it that much more fun to learn!

We’re a sandbox grooming the capital markets investors of tomorrow, while triggering ideas for the investors of today.

Our Team

TradeHero is built by former wall street trading platform geeks, mobile gaming gurus from Electronic Arts, and seasoned startup athletes.

Investors

MyHero is the parent company of TradeHero and our investors are Kleiner Perkins Caufield and Byers (KPCB China) and IPV Capital.

Partners

We partner with stock exchanges, brokerages, and banks to grow the financial community, leveraging the TH platform.

We also partner with leading content developers and curators to bring you the most relevant bite sized news and insights.
